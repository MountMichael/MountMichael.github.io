My personal work


I wouldn't be trying hard enough if I didn't have something to show for myself.
Some of my origonal work was focused on renewable resources on my college campus, and I had the privlage of doing it with my good friend Ian Schwing.
We applied a minimum bucket system to our school's prexisting solar resources so that we could quantify what was needed for clean energy and independence.


A photo of us presenting our research!

This project was really fun an inspired me to stay focused on the outdoors for reaserch and projects. So my Honor's project and senior poster are both focused on the essential elements of mapping trails.
